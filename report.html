Inspiration

The website is dedicated to promoting financial wellbeing for women. Many women, even those with substantial earnings, are not investing. A common reason cited for not investing is a lack of time and fear of the associated risks. But if we shift our perspective, we realize there's a risk in not investing too: the devaluation of savings due to inflation.

Financial wellbeing is about more than just money; it's about freedom. Freedom from jobs or partners that may not align with one's ideal life. It's crucial to understand and steadily work towards achieving this form of wellbeing, and that's the message we aim to convey through our website.

  • First Page (Home)

    This page defines what financial wellbeing means. It also features the financial coach's own story, highlighting her personal realization about the importance of investing when she and her husband were looking to buy their dream home.

  • Second Page (Course)

    This page provides details about courses aimed at enhancing women's financial management skills. The highlight is a 6-week course comprised of 5 sessions, detailing what participants can learn and what they can expect.

  • Third Page (Resource)

    This is an option for those who may not wish to take the course. It provides a list of resources, such as books and podcasts, to facilitate independent learning about financial management.

  • Fourth Page (News Letter)

    This is dedicated to the newsletter. Our financial coach posts daily, encouraging women to stay cognizant of their financial wellbeing. This page highlights key messages and provides links to an archive of all posts.

  • Fifth Page (Call to Action)

    After grasping the significance of financial wellbeing and comparing the two learning routes (self-study vs. supported learning), and being further enlightened by our Instagram posts, users can make one of three choices:

    • Subscribe to the newsletter.
    • Book a free introductory chat with the coach.
    • Reserve a seat for the course.
